ABOUT

LXDOVICO (a.k.a. Ludo) is a flutist and producer based in London with international background in classical and electronic music production, with more than 10 years of experience in classical training in Milan.

He is actively collaborating and performing with Luzai in Italy and Sega Bodega in UK as a flutist, musician and recording musician, as well as working on his own projects exploring queer experimental electronic music.

Inspired by Björk and FKA Twigs, as well as traditional folk and hypnotic trance, Ludo explores his roots in Apulia, South Italy’s tarantism rhythms and the industrial and alienating sounds of London with the sound of the flute and his hybrid-electronic productions.
