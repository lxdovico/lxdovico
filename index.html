Ludo (a.k.a. LXDOVICO) is a flutist and producer based in London, working across classical and electronic music with over a decade of training and performance experience, including studies in Milan.

He collaborates as a musician and performer with artists and projects in both Italy and the UK, including work with Sega Bodega. Alongside collaborations, he develops his own hybrid sound, blending experimental electronic production with the flute.

Inspired by queer club music, traditional folk and hypnotic trance, Ludo draws from his roots in Apulia, South Italy (including the rhythmic traditions of tarantism) as well as the industrial and alienating textures of urban spaces like London and Milan. His work explores a dialogue between organic and synthetic sound, merging experimental flute practices with electronic production to create dystopian landscapes.
