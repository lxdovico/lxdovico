Hi! I'm Ludo, a producer and flutist based in London, working across experimental and electronic music with over 10 years of classical training and performance experience (flute), as well as studies in electronic music production in Milan and London.

I collaborate as a musician and performer with artists and projects in both Italy and the UK, including work with Sega Bodega, Luzai and Bishi. Alongside collaborations, I'm developing my own hybrid sound blending experimental electronic production with the flute.
